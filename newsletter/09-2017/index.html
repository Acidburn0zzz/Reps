Firefox Quantum Sprint
The Firefox Quantum Sprint is here! A few events have happened and over 800 websites have already been tested. You can find a list of all events and a guide on how to run this activity online on the Sprint website. This is a common effort from several Mozilla communities, resulting in over 100 events in more than 40 countries!

Reps Resources Training

The Resources Training for Indian Reps is currently ongoing. We reopened the applications for the other areas and recently announced 14 further selections. The training for the rest of the world will start soon. With this initiative we can train Reps on effectively guiding Reps in all Resources matter by guiding them on how to set up an impactful event and requesting budget for them.

Reps Coaching Training

All Reps can take the Reps Coaching training on their own pace and 7 Reps have done so already. We encourage all Reps with interest in coaching to go through the training and to write their narrative on Discourse. We will soon organize the first Practice Week to further deepen the learnings with practice examples and conversations. This will act as final step of the training. If you haven't already, head over to the training and get started.
