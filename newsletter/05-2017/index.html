Onboarding new Reps

As you know, we have started our on-boarding Webinar experiment at the beginning of April. We are happy to have welcomed 15 new Reps in their Orientation Period so far. In the middle of May we pushed the on-boarding experiment to a new level, inviting 12 more Reps into the program. As these Reps go through the Webinar, we are confident that the Webinar is a good resource to on-board new Reps in a scaling matter.

Update on MozActivate
Thanks for all your contributions to MozActivate! These are high impact activities we encourage everyone to mobilize for. Additionally to the Impact Form on Google Forms you can also directly point your attendees to the MozActivate website to fill out the feedback form. We are currently working on having a separate page where you can see your impact. This will help you to validate what type of attendees you had, your NPS score as well as the number of responses you got.
