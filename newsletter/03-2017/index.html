Reps of the Month

February 2017

Please join us in congratulating Siddhartha Rao and Vishal Chavan, our Reps of the Month for February 2017!  Read more
Siddhartha

Siddhartha is a Computer Engineering major from New York, he is also a technology leader with rich experience in Security, Web Development and Data Privacy Evangelism. He started with his Mozilla contribution by leading the Firefox Club and Cyber Cell for a year at his university. Sid was a speaker and Data Privacy Ingeniouses at the Glassroom initiative by Tactical Tech and Mozilla.
Vishal

Vishal is a web entrepreneur and a champion Mozilla contributor since April 2013. He started his Mozilla journey by organizing sessions focused on teaching the web to school and university students. He then became a part of Mozilla Foundation when assigned the role of a Regional Coordinator for Mozilla Clubs. One of the key roles he played as a contributor was being a core team member who started the January Privacy Month Campaign. A campaign which, as of 2017, has proven to be a global success for the third year in a row.

New Activate Front Page
We've changed the Activate front page and it looks better than ever!
In detail what we did is:
  • We removed the categories
  • Activities are now shown on the frontpage for easy access
  • There is a slider at the bottom of the frontpage to give you tips on where to start
  • Activities have a tag on the bottom left of the box to indicate what skill set they belong to (for now "Programming", "Participation" and "Testing")
  • Those tags are also indicated by the border color of the box
  • Activities now have nice pictures to quickly identify them
  • The suggested duration of each activity can now be seen without opening it
  • We changed the description and header picture
 
Check it out!

 
Mozilla Developer Program Survey
Mozilla is working on a new program to connect and support web developers and web builders who share Mozilla's values and we're trying to reach as many developers as we can to understand what they think of this concept, what value it would bring to them, how they would like to be involved, etc.

Our ask: Help expand the reach of our research by sharing this survey with all of the web developers in your networks!

Want to know more about this project? Join us in the Reps call on the 6th of April where Jeremie Patonnier from the developer marketing team will be sharing more about the project and the survey.
